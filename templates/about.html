About The Project Of

Machine Learning Based Assessment of Mental Stress using Wearable Sensor Data

Healthcare Monitoring and Intervention

Real-time Stress Monitoring: Wearable devices can continuously monitor physiological signals (heart rate, skin temperature, etc.) and provide real-time assessment of an individual's stress levels. This can help in early detection of stress-related disorders and prompt intervention.

Personalized Stress Management: By analyzing data over time, machine learning models can offer personalized insights and recommendations for managing stress, like suggesting relaxation exercises, physical activities, or meditation.

Workplace Wellness Programs

Employee Well-being: Companies can implement these systems to monitor the stress levels of employees. By detecting elevated stress in real time, employers can offer support, adjust workloads, or initiate wellness programs to reduce workplace stress.

Productivity Optimization: Understanding employees’ stress patterns can help employers design better work schedules or identify high-stress periods where productivity might decrease, allowing for better workload management.

Mental Health Applications

Therapy Assistance: Mental health professionals can use data-driven insights from wearable sensors to better understand a patient’s stress patterns, improve diagnosis, and provide personalized treatment options for anxiety, depression, or chronic stress.

Preventive Care: Early detection of high stress levels can help in preventing more serious mental health conditions, such as anxiety or burnout, enabling early intervention and reducing the need for more intensive treatment.

Sports and Fitness

Athlete Stress Monitoring: Stress levels during training and competition can impact an athlete’s performance. Wearable sensors can help coaches and athletes assess mental and physical stress levels, optimizing recovery periods and improving overall performance.

Fitness Apps: Integrating stress level monitoring into fitness apps can help users balance their workout routines with relaxation, promoting a healthier lifestyle.
